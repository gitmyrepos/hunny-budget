Main Features

Budget
  • Split up by Income, Expenses, Savings and Spending.
  • Build multiple budgets and compare them.
 Calendar
  • See your upcoming bills and paychecks.
  • Filter only bills or any items you want to see.
 Goals
  • Set goals for everything big and small.
  • Set amount aside each week and see your savings grow.
 Accounts
  • Mirror your bank accounts to help see your budget in action.
  • See the flow of money through your bank accounts to match your main budget.
